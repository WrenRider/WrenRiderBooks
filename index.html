About Wren Rider

Wren Rider writes atmospheric, character-driven fiction. Their work explores the quiet mysteries of loyalty, memory, and rediscovery. Choosing a more mysterious presence, Wren invites readers to step into stories that reveal as much about themselves as the characters they follow.
